## ðŸŽ¯ Key Takeaways

1. **Personalization is Critical**: Each learner type needs different pacing, support, and challenge levels

2. **Flow State Varies**: High-achievers need challenge to maintain flow; strugglers need support to enter flow

3. **Bloom Progression is Adaptive**: Advanced students can jump to higher levels quickly; struggling students need foundation building

4. **Spaced Repetition is Tailored**: Review intervals and difficulty adjustments vary significantly by learner profile

5. **Feynman Quality Improves with Support**: All students improve explanation quality, but at different rates and with different support levels

6. **Foundation Matters**: Struggling students benefit from very short review intervals and lots of repetition before progressing
